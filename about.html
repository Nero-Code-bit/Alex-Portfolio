ABOUT ME

Hello I'm ALEX

My name is Jared Alexandrei P. Lita (or just Alex), and I’m a web designer and front-end developer with a passion for creating visually engaging and user-friendly digital experiences. Based in Davao City, Philippines, I have a keen eye for design and a love for coding that drives me to build websites that not only look great but also function seamlessly.

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Jose Maria College, where I continue to grow my skills in web design, front-end development, and all things tech.

I specialize in building responsive, accessible, and modern websites that blend clean design with functional code. Whether I’m fine-tuning layouts with HTML and CSS or adding interactivity with JavaScript, I love bringing ideas to life through thoughtful, creative solutions.

I believe that creativity, curiosity, and empathy are just as important as technical skills. These values guide not only how I design, but also how I collaborate and grow as a developer. I'm always eager to learn new tools, take on challenges, and connect with others who share the same passion.

Let’s build something amazing together.
